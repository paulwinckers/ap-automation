Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 27, 2026

This application ("AP Automation") is an internal tool operated by Darios Landscaping for the sole purpose of processing vendor invoices and employee expense submissions.

Information We Collect

We collect invoice documents (PDFs and images), vendor information, and employee names submitted through this application. This data is used exclusively for accounts payable processing within Darios Landscaping.

How We Use Your Information

Invoice and expense data is processed using the Anthropic Claude API for data extraction and is routed to QuickBooks Online for accounting purposes. No data is sold or shared with third parties outside of these integrations.

Data Storage

Submitted invoices and associated data are stored securely using Cloudflare infrastructure and are accessible only to authorized Darios Landscaping staff.

Contact

For questions regarding this privacy policy, contact paul@darios.ca.
